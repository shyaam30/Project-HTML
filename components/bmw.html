From a company that manufactured aircraft engines, BMW has evolved into one of the top producers of not only some of Europe's finest luxury cars but also some of the very best motorcycles, raking in 6 figures in yearly sales. BMW has been producing motorcycles since 1923. The first BMW bike, the R32, ran on an 8.5 hp 494cc flat-twin engine and had a top speed of 59 mph. In 2011, their two millionth bike—an R 1200 GS—was built. Today, the company's motorcycles are light-years away in terms of design, performance, equipment, and functions from what they used to be. BMW motorcycles are perfectly engineered and technologically advanced machines that are well-equipped to give riders the ultimate experience. There is something from the stables of BMW for everyone: from tourers, adventure bikes, classic cruisers, and sportbikes to scooters.
